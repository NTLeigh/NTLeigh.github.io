Something About Myself

  • Welcome to my website!
  • I am a bachelor in Biomedical Engineering from Zhejiang University, China. I’m interested in visualization from every aspects, including the traditional medical imaging and the data visualization. From tiny molecules in cell and human body, to massive data like public transportation, I like to interpret those abstract and bewildering data into more direct manner and find out something special within, or to restore the seemingly unusable data to reconstruct the actual image.
  • Fields of Interest: Bionanotechnology; Computer Graphics; Medical Imaging; Neural Network; Deep learning.
